Frequently Asked Questions

⇨ What is Manga?


Manga (漫画) are comics created in Japan, or by Japanese creators in the Japanese language, conforming to a style developed in Japan in the late 19th century.
They have a long and complex pre-history in earlier Japanese art.

Learn More

⇨ How does Manga-Mania work?


Manga-Mania is planned to be a paid service involving one-time purchasing of material which is then kept digitally by the consumer forever and protected under our terms. Any such material witnessed being then distributed will have copyright sanctions against the responsible party.

⇨ What are the benefits?


If you purchase from Manga-Mania, we guarantee the highest quality digital viewing of Manga. Besides this, we want to ensure the creators get the entirety of their profit, most of which is lost through piracy or other means.

⇨ What are the effects of piracy?


For example, a credible analysis by the Institute for Policy Innovation concluded that global music piracy causes $12.5 Billion of economic losses every year. While we're nowhere near that, we hold to heart every genre of artist should be protect against such losses.

⇨ How recent do you release Manga?


As soon as possible. We provide purely the english translated variants and are on the fence as to whether or not we could enlist translations of our own for different regions.

⇨ When will Manga-Mania be available?


We are aiming for an international availability release in the third quarter of this year. Anything is possible, maybe even sooner! Top keep up-to-date, like our Facebook page or follow us on Twitter using the links in our navbar or the former text. :)
